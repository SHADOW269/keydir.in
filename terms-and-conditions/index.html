SECTION_005
Directory Data Disclaimer
Read Before Buying
💡
KEYDIR.in is a community directory, not a verified review platform. Always do your own research before purchasing from any listed vendor.

The information in this directory is contributed and maintained by community members. While we make reasonable efforts to keep entries accurate, we make no guarantees about the completeness, correctness, or timeliness of any data.

Directory data may:

  • ⚠Change over time — vendors open, close, or change their product offerings without notice
  • ⚠Contain mistakes — URLs, categories, or descriptions may be incorrect or outdated
  • ⚠Vary from your actual experience — product availability, pricing, and quality may differ from what is implied
  • ⚠Omit vendors — this is not an exhaustive list of all keyboard vendors operating in India

Entries marked "Enter at own risk" reflect community-reported concerns about a vendor's reliability, support quality, or business practices. These flags are based on community reports and do not constitute a formal review or legal finding.

You must independently verify any vendor or product before making a purchase. Check reviews on Reddit (r/mkindia), Discord, and other community sources before spending money.
