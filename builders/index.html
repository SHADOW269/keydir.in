// WHAT IS A DIY BUILDER?

DIY builders are specialized artisans focused on unique, custom keyboard components — not mass-produced gear.

They offer things like custom coiled cables, artisan keycaps, lubing services, foam modding, and bespoke keyboard assembly.

If you want your build to be genuinely one-of-a-kind, start here.

WHY GO CUSTOM?

🎯
Precision
Custom builders obsess over details that mass manufacturers ignore — switch films, lubing consistency, PCB flex, sound dampening.
🇮🇳
Local Support
Supporting Indian builders means faster turnarounds, no international shipping fees, and a growing local keyboard ecosystem.
⭐
Uniqueness
Your build will be genuinely one-of-a-kind. No two custom boards are the same — that's the whole point.
