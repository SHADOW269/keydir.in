// WHAT IS A DIY BUILDER?

DIY builders are independent creators and specialists who provide custom services beyond what traditional vendors offer.

Their work can include custom keyboard builds, switch lubing and filming, keyboard modding, repairs and PCB work, artisan keycaps, custom cables, and other personalized keyboard services.

Whether you're building your first custom keyboard or upgrading an existing one, these builders can help bring your ideal setup to life.
