KNOW YOUR SURFACE

3 TYPES
🔲
Mousepad
200mm – 450mm wide
Single-mouse-zone pads. Compact, portable, and focused. Cloth top with rubber base. Available in speed (smooth) and control (textured) variants. The everyday essential.
📋
Deskpad
800mm – 1200mm wide
Extended desk mats that cover both keyboard and mouse. Protects desk surface, reduces noise, unifies your setup aesthetic. Popular with keyboard enthusiasts for the clean, cohesive look.
◻️
Glasspad
Specialized surface
Tempered glass gaming surface. Ultra-low friction for laser/optical sensors. Immune to wear, easily cleaned, never loses glide. Harsh on some sensor types — check compatibility.

SIZE GUIDE

WHAT TO PICK
// STANDARD PAD SIZES
APPROX. DIMENSIONS
Mouse · S
200 × 200 mm
Tiny. Fits on the tightest desks. For claw-grip players and low-sensitivity setups.
Mouse · M
350 × 300 mm
The sweet spot for most gamers. Enough room for mid-sensitivity FPS play.
Mouse · XL
450 × 400 mm
Popular size — covers full mouse zone with room. Good for low-sensitivity gamers.
Desk · S
800 × 400 mm
Covers keyboard + mouse zone for 60–75% boards. Standard "extended" size.
Desk · L
900 × 400 mm
Fits TKL + full mouse zone. Recommended for most keyboard + mouse setups.
Desk · XL
1200 × 600 mm
Full desk coverage. For large desks or those who want maximum protection + aesthetics.
Glass
Varies
Usually mousepad-sized (M–XL). Check vendor listing for exact dimensions.
