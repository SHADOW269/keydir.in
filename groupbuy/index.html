WHAT'S A GROUP BUY?

3 STAGES
🟢
Active
Interest check or payment window is open right now. You can still join — check the MOQ progress and deadline before paying.
🔵
Upcoming
Announced but not yet open for orders. Follow the organizer's Discord or store page to get notified when it opens.
⚪
Closed
Order window has ended. Outcome shown — delivered, in production, or refunded. Kept here as a historical record for trust-checking organizers.
